Biography

Junior Clementino is a Master’s candidate at the Intermidia Lab of the University of São Paulo. His Interests include distributed systems, blockchain, data mining, IoT, and security. He leads a research related to blockchain in healthcare environments to improve privacy using differential privacy, supervised by Prof. Jó Ueyama.
